Local Universe Reading Group (back to ROE service)

The Local Universe Reading Group is a science group at the Institue for Astronomy focused on all things nearer than the Virgo Cluster (our nominal definition of 'local'). We meet every other week, typically in the Stobie room.

Reviews

During 2020, we worked our way through review papers as a group. Two resulted in some level of interactivity for data:

Simon: The Faintest Dwarf Galaxies

Greene: Intermediate mass black holes
