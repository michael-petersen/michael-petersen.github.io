Equality, Diversity, and Inclusion

The Equality, Diversity, and Inclusion group at the Institute for Astronomy has the following goals:
-Make the IfA an inclusive and welcoming space
-Recognize the experience of feeling excluded
-Identify and improve on current problem areas
-Apply lessons broadly to attack systemic barriers preventing inclusion.

We meet twice a month for an hour for discussion sessions and programming meetings. Please contact michael.petersen@roe.ac.uk if interested in joining our mailing list!
