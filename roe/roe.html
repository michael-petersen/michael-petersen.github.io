Royal Observatory Edinburgh Service notes

At the Royal Observatory Edinburgh and the Institute for Astronomy, I've been engaged with several initiatives. I eventually figured out that I needed a public holding location for some of these activities that I could easily share. This is the landing page for those initiatives.

Activities (click headings to go to dedicated pages!)

Sonification

I'm involved in a project representing images of galaxies as sounds. As a first demonstration, I took some JWST galaxies and converted them to pedagogical demonstrations of the sonification. Please check it out and send me any feedback!


Theory Lunch

I've done work organising the Institute for Astronomy's 'Theory Lunch', Wednesdays at 1pm.


Equality, Diversity, and Inclusion (EDI)

I actively participate in the IfA's grassroots EDI effort, co-organising monthly discussion groups.


Local Universe Reading Group (LURG)

I formerly co-organised the Local Universe Reading Group.


Seminar Organisation

I was part of the team organising main scientific seminars for the Royal Observatory Edinburgh from 2019-2021.


[Other]

I'm a compulsive data-keeper. I have many notes from applying to positions in several job cycles, and I have details about my experiences running student projects. I'm very happy to share, just contact me!
