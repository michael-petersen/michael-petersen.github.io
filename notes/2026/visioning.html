What comes next?

1 January 2026

I'm in a somewhat precarious position now for employment as a professional astronomer. Given that I've been bouncing from contract to contract for a while now, this isn't particularly surprising, but to prepare for future steps, I'm thinking about what I really like about academia, and research, and projects. These notes have been helpful for looking back at -- even if I've historically left them a bit vague.


Over the next couple of months, I'm going to grow the content on this page as a challenge to myself to produce some projects that I care about. I have some ideas, but I won't yet fully commit myself to them.
