Bicycle Commuting

I've been regularly commuting by bicycle for a number of years, but I finally kicked it up a notch after moving to Edinburgh, with no more car in the picture. The University of Edinburgh has a reasonably generous cycle-to-work scheme that can help defray the upfront cost of purchasing a bicycle.
