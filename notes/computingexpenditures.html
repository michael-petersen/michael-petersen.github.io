How much computing budget do I need?

(This post is backdated to when I switched to my current setup, but it features more recent updates.)


I fully admit that I'm a bit weird about spending grant money and new computers. I've personally purchased both of the laptops that I use for science research (although I'm a huge proponent of shared resources, and computing clusters), and I use the computers as workhorses for absolutely everything I want to do with a computer. I fully admit that I waited too long to upgrade my first graduate school laptop, which I write more about below.


To that end, I try to both (a) keep compulsive track of how much I'm spending, and (b) try to understand as much as possible why I am investing in something. I'm also counting all my computer peripherals.

Current setup (2015-)


I'm currently on a MacBook Pro (Retina, 13-inch, Early 2015). I ordered this machine on April 27th, 2015. I purchased this machine online, for $1,878.

Previous setup (2010-2015)


From June 2010 to May 2015, I was on a MacBook Pro (13-inch, Early 2010). I purchased this machine for $1,049.
