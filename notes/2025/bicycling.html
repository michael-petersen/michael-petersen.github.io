Bicycle Commuting II

30 March 2025

(this post talks about my most recent bicycle, if you are interested in my earlier bicycle, see here.)


After the sad end to my first Edinburgh bicycle, I purchased a 2023 Cube Attain Road Bicycle on 30 March 2025. Because this was well after the date the bicycle was new, it was discounted from £999 to £499.


Some stream-of-consciousness thoughts: the larger bicycle means it is harder to ride up out of the saddle, as I already have a more upright posture while riding. The 2023 edition of the Attain still features quick-release wheels; this is something to keep in mind when needing to replace them (as happened on the old bicycle).
