Printable orbit files

These are 3D printable files of different types of orbits. You can also open these using (e.g.) Preview on Apple computers and interact with them in 3d.


Orbit 1: Low-order commensurability.

Orbit 2: Medium-complicated orbit.

Orbit 3: Non-commensurate orbit.

Artemis 2: The Artemis 2 mission trajectory.

I am also happy to provide a printer-ready file for laying out the orbits on a print bed. Printing all three of these orbits on a Bambu XS1 took 7 hours. Blender (which I used to prepare the stl files) did not attach meaningful units to the print, I should look into this more. I used Bambu slicer to render the actual sliced models for printing.
